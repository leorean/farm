DESIGN:
* you are a daywalker (a vampire who can walkin daylight)
* at day, you are a humble farmer
* at night, you turn into a vampire
* using tools costs stamina
* getting hit costs stamina
* if you used all your stamina, you get knocked out and end up in your bed the next morning
* the goal of the game is to turn every person into a vampire
	* in order to do that, you must gift each person 3 of their favorite gifts in order to gain their friendship
	* once you have friendship level 3 with them, they will allow you to turn them
	* for each person you turned into a vampire, you will gain a level-up
* when you level up, you can assign a skill point (SP)
* through SP, you can level the following:
	* max stamina
	* attack damage
	* life leech (advanced)
	* stamina leech (advanced)
	* certain skills
		* swim
		* dive
		* jump
		* fly
		* teleport
